Body Frame Size

  • Small Frame: Wrist circumference < 16.5 cm
  • Medium Frame: Wrist 16.5-18.5 cm
  • Large Frame: Wrist > 18.5 cm

Healthy Weight Tips

  • Consult healthcare providers for guidance
