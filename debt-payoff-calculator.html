Debt Avalanche

The Debt Avalanche method prioritizes paying off debts with the highest interest rates first. This strategy minimizes the total interest paid and typically results in the fastest payoff time.

Debt Snowball

The Debt Snowball method focuses on paying off the smallest debts first. While it may cost more in interest, the psychological boost from quick wins can help maintain motivation.

Extra Payments

Making extra payments, even small ones, can significantly reduce your total interest paid and help you become debt-free faster. Consider allocating windfalls or bonuses to debt repayment.
