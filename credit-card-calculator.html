Understanding APR

APR (Annual Percentage Rate) is the yearly interest rate charged on outstanding balances. A lower APR means you'll pay less in interest over time.

Minimum Payments

Making only minimum payments can significantly extend your payoff time and increase total interest paid. Try to pay more than the minimum whenever possible.

Extra Payments

Even small extra payments can make a big difference in reducing your total interest and payoff time. Consider allocating windfalls or bonuses to credit card payments.
